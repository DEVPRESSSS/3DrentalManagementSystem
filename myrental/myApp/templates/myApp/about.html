Welcome to QQ Mall

Company's Overview

Founded in 2003, QQ Mall has been a cornerstone of the Quiapo community, providing affordable rental spaces for tenants and entrepreneurs. prime location at #725 Quezon Blvd. Zone 030 Brgy. 308, Quiapo, Manila, Philippines makes us an ideal choice for businesses looking to thrive. With our strategic proximity to Isetan, Sun Star Hotel, Quiapo Church, and several universities, QQ Mall is a hub of activity and opportunity.

Our Mission

At QQ Mall, our mission is to foster a vibrant marketplace where businesses can flourish. We strive to offer accessible rental options that empower entrepreneurs and provide customers with a diverse shopping experience.

Our Vision

We envision QQ Mall as a leading shopping destination that not only supports local businesses but also enriches the community through innovation and collaboration.

Community Engagement

We believe in building strong relationships with our tenants and the surrounding community.

Affordability

Providing cost-effective rental solutions is at the heart of our operations.

Integrity

We conduct our business with transparency and honesty.
